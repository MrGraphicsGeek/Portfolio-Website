About Me

My name is Christopher Henyard. I'm a beginning web designer from the San Gabriel Valley.

I've been drawing and sketching things for as long as I can remember, and was interested in starting a career involving computers from an early age.

I graduated from La Joya Community High school in Avondale, AZ in 2017. It was then that I attended nearby Estrella Mountain Community College from 2017 to 2019, where I majored in web development and graphic design.

When I'm not working, my hobbies include PC gaming, 3D modeling and watching NASCAR.
